Welcome to TrainingBazaar!

A marketplace where users can loan their computers for training machine learning models in exchange for payment.

Get in touch

Are you a machine learning (ML) researcher or developer looking to speed up your training times? Do you have idle computers that you’d like to put to use and earn some extra cash? If so, TrainingBazaar is for you!

TrainingBazaar is a marketplace where ML researchers and developers can rent idle computers from individuals and organizations to use for training their models. Simply write your training code, specify the number of computers you need, and let TrainingBazaar handle the rest!

How it works

  1. Register your computer on TrainingBazaar and specify your availability and pricing.
  2. Request training from TrainingBazaar by specifying the number of computers you need, your training script, and your dataset.
  3. TrainingBazaar will deploy your training environment, script, and dataset to the specified computers and start the training process.
  4. When the training is complete, TrainingBazaar will stop the training and compute the billing or payment distribution report based on the usage of each computer.
  5. You will receive the training results and the billing or payment distribution report via email.

Benefits

  • Fast training times: TrainingBazaar allows you to rent multiple computers to speed up your training times.
  • Easy setup: TrainingBazaar handles the deployment and setup of the training environment, script, and dataset, so you can focus on writing your code.
  • Fair pricing: TrainingBazaar uses a transparent pricing model based on the usage of each computer, so you only pay for what you use.

Our Story

TrainingBazaar was founded by a group of machine learning researchers and developers who were frustrated with the long training times for their models. They often had idle computers at their disposal, but no easy way to put them to use for training.

At the same time, they knew that there were many other researchers and developers who were also struggling with slow training times and had idle computers that could be put to use.

So, they came up with the idea for TrainingBazaar: a marketplace where ML researchers and developers could rent idle computers from individuals and organizations to use for training their models.

With TrainingBazaar, researchers and developers could easily request training on multiple computers, and the owners of the idle computers could earn some extra cash by renting out their computers when they weren’t being used.

The founders of TrainingBazaar knew that this was a win-win situation: researchers and developers could speed up their training times, and computer owners could earn some extra money. And so, TrainingBazaar was born.

Since its launch, TrainingBazaar has become a popular and trusted platform for ML researchers and developers looking to speed up their training times, and for computer owners looking to earn some extra cash by renting out their idle computers.
