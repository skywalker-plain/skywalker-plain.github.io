Digital Prescriptions App for Doctors

$6.00 / month with a 15-day free trial

This app allows doctors to create beautiful prescriptions, consultation receipts and patient database in their own letterheads and format by simply speaking into it.

Just speak the name, age, and prescriptions into the app and in a click you get a beautiful digital prescription in your letterhead, a consultation receipt, a patient database, and many more.

The prescriptions, receipts, and excel file get uploaded to your google drive as data backup.

You can easily search previous prescriptions and receipts.
