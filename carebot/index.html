Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is CareBot?

CareBot is an AI-powered platform that utilizes chatbot technology to streamline medical practice. It offers features such as patient history collection, prescription generation, medication reminders, and practice management tools to enhance efficiency and improve patient care.

Is CareBot HIPAA compliant?

Yes, CareBot is fully compliant with HIPAA regulations. We prioritize data security, privacy, and confidentiality, ensuring that patient information is protected and handled in accordance with HIPAA guidelines.

How secure is patient data on CareBot?

CareBot prioritizes data security. We utilize robust encryption technologies, secure servers, and regular security audits to safeguard patient data. Your patients’ sensitive information is protected from unauthorized access or breaches. Rather, all the patient’s data sits with you, not with us, it is on your Google drive.

Can I access CareBot from my mobile device?

Yes, CareBot is designed to be accessible from mobile devices, allowing you to manage your practice on the go. Our platform is mobile-responsive, ensuring a seamless user experience across different devices.

Can I customize CareBot to suit my practice’s needs?

Absolutely! CareBot offers customization options to tailor the platform to your practice’s unique requirements. From personalized letterheads to custom reminders and workflows, we work with you to ensure CareBot aligns with your practice’s branding and workflows.
